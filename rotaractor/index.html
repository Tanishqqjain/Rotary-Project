Rotaract Club of Marathalli

1 2 3
Rotaract club of Marathalli under the guidance of parent club Rotary Bangalore Midtown was started in the year 2015 . Since then the team members have worked continuously on community service projects in line with leadership training for under privileged , health checkup’s of the needy , and spreading awareness about sustainable living .
