Rotaractor Club of Mount Carmel College

Rotaract Club of Mount Carmel College is one of the oldest clubs in the district 3190. The team comprises of more than 300 incredible women who have worked extensively for the betterment of the society. Women at Rotaract Club of Mount Carmel College work with the vision of helping the underprivileged and doing their best to make our planet a better place by doing projects highlighting- recycling, fundraiser for providing the underprivileged, health checkups etc.
